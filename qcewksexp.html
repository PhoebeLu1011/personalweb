My First International Conference Presentation at QCE25 !

I was honored to present our paper “Quantum-Enhanced Reinforcement Learning with LSTM Forecasting Signals for Optimizing Fintech Trading Decisions” at IEEE QCE25 – International Workshop on Quantum Computing Reinforcement Learning. This work was co-authored with Yen-Ku Liu, Yun-Huei Pan, Pei-Fan Lu, Yun-Cheng Tsai, and Samuel Yen-Chi Chen. Grateful for the chance to contribute as a co-author and to share our work with the community.
